# Karu Porã — programa de alimentación saludable

> **TL;DR:** Healthy-food program operating under Ñande Rekorã. Distributes food + nutritional support to community members, particularly those living with HIV or facing food insecurity. Recipients include DNIT-donated supplies (April 2024).

---

## What it is

- Healthy-food program
- Operates as a sub-program of Ñande Rekorã
- Distributes food to community members, especially:
  - People living with HIV (nutrition is critical for TARV adherence)
  - Low-income LGBTQ+ community members
  - Youth shelter residents
- Receives in-kind donations from govt + private donors

---

## Evidence

- **April 2024:** DNIT donated food + supplies specifically to Karu Porã alongside Ñande Rekorã
  - Source: https://somosgay.org/news/DNIT-realiza-donacin-solidaria-a-la-Clnica-Kunuu-y-al-Programa-Karu-Por
