# Besatón y Marcha del Orgullo — public events

> **TL;DR:** Two annual public events. **Besatón** = a kiss-in protest in front of the Paraguayan Parliament (Plaza frente al Congreso). **Marcha del Orgullo** = Asunción Pride, growing year over year per Paloma Vera (ABC Color 2024). Both co-organised with AIREANA.

---

## Besatón (annual)

- **Type:** Kiss-in protest
- **Location:** Plaza in front of the Paraguayan Parliament
- **Frequency:** Annual
- **Origin:** Per IBA Reporter (2019): *"SOMOSGAY helps organise an annual Besaton (kiss-in) in a public square in front of Parliament"*
- **Purpose:** Visible public protest against discrimination
- **Political context:** Held in the seat of legislative power; a direct visual counter to the education bans + anti-marriage-equality legislation

---

## Marcha del Orgullo — Asunción Pride

- **Type:** Pride parade
- **Location:** Asunción (typically through the historic centre)
- **Frequency:** Annual (June, around Pride month — though Paraguay's local date may differ)
- **Co-organisers:** SOMOSGAY + AIREANA
- **Growth:** Per Paloma Vera (ABC Color 2024): *"hay un cambio social muy importante, lo que es visible en las 'paradas del orgullo', que cada vez son más grandes, cada vez hay más gente que sale a la calle"*
  - Translation: *"there is a very important social change, which is visible in the 'Pride parades', which are bigger and bigger, more and more people come out to the street"*
