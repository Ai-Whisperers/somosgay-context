# Centro Comunitario Tekoharã

> **TL;DR:** The community centre at Independencia Nacional 1032. Used for workshops, talks, training, university outreach, testing events. "Tekoharã" — Guaraní, likely meaning related to "tekoha" (place of being / dwelling place). Same physical building as Clínica Kunu'u.

---

## What it is

- Drop-in community space at Independencia Nacional 1032, Asunción
- Hosts:
  - Workshops + talks
  - Community testing events (HIV, syphilis, Hep B)
  - University outreach (e.g., students of UNISAL Psychology, Sep 2023)
  - First-aid training (Red Cross partnership, March 2024)
  - LGBTQ+ youth programming
  - Volunteer coordination
  - Material distribution (condoms, lube, autotest kits, info pamphlets)

## Historical context

The physical space has served as Asunción's LGBTQ+ community centre since the org's founding in 2009. The IBA Reporter (2019) describes it:

> *"Despite unending opposition from five conservative governments since its inception, SOMOSGAY thrives. Its programmes include a health clinic and a drop-in centre in its equally colourful, well-appointed space. SOMOSGAY helps organise an annual Besaton (kiss-in) in a public square in front of Parliament and the annual Pride parade in Asunción. It produces many attractive publications, full of updated information about LGBTQ+ rights and issues like HIV."*

## Youth shelter (related, off-site)

40 minutes outside Asunción is a residential shelter for LGBTQ+ youth kicked out by their families. Per IBA Reporter (2019):

- Two-story residence around a central courtyard
- Fountain decorated with 108 rainbow tiles
- Support columns painted rainbow
- Capacity ~5 teens + 1 chaperone when visited
- Off-site for safety + privacy
