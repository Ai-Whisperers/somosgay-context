# Ñande Rekorã — sistema de cuidado comunitario

> **TL;DR:** "Ñande Rekorã" — Guaraní, likely meaning "our way" or "our path/mode." A community-care system that wraps multiple programs (clinic, food, social support, mental health, harm reduction) into a coordinated care network. Recipient of DNIT food + supply donation in April 2024.

---

## What it is

A coordinated community-care system, distinct from individual programs but encompassing them:
- Connects Kunu'u clinic patients to social support
- Distributes food + supplies via Karu Porã
- Hosts mental-health programming
- Likely the operational backbone for chronic care, peer support, and case management

---

## Evidence

- **April 2024:** DNIT (Dirección General de Ingresos Tributarios) donated food + supplies to *"el sistema de cuidado comunitario Ñande Rekorã y su programa de alimentación saludable"*
  - Source: https://somosgay.org/news/DNIT-realiza-donacin-solidaria-a-la-Clnica-Kunuu-y-al-Programa-Karu-Por

---

## Why this name was chosen

"Nuestra forma" / "nuestro modo" — Guaraní framing consistent with the org's *tekoporã* (good living) worldview. Aligns with how the org positions itself culturally: Paraguayan-first, rooted in Guaraní heritage, not a foreign-style NGO.
