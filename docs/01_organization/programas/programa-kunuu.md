# Programa Kunu'u — campaigns + community prevention

> **TL;DR:** Public-health campaign arm of the org. Runs the "Yo ❤️ PrEP: Yo Amo Más Seguro" campaign (Dec 2023) and the "Comunidad en Prevención Combinada: Cuidando nuestra Salud Mental" series (March 2024). Funded/backed by OPS/OMS, Red GayLatino, PRONASIDA, MSPBS. Targets gay/bi men + trans people.

---

## "Yo ❤️ PrEP: Yo Amo Más Seguro" (2023)

Launched for World AIDS Day (Dec 1) 2023. Backed by:
- OPS/OMS (PAHO/WHO)
- SOMOSGAY
- Red GayLatino

Stated goal: integrate PrEP into daily life of gay/bisexual/trans community in Paraguay.

Message framing: "amor propio y cuidado comunitario" (self-love + community care).

Includes illustrated educational materials targeting the community.

Source: https://www.abc.com.py/nacionales/2023/11/20/lanzan-campana-del-dia-de-la-respuesta-al-vih-yo-amo-prep-yo-amo-mas-seguro/

## "Comunidad en Prevención Combinada: Cuidando nuestra Salud Mental" (2024)

Launched March 2024. Focus: mental health as part of combination prevention.

New materials released to address and better understand the components of combination prevention + mental-health care.

Source: https://somosgay.org/news/Nuevos-materiales-de-la-campaa-Comunidad-en-Prevencin-Combinada-Cuidando-nuestra-Salud-Mental

## Combination prevention strategy (the org's stated framework)

Per their own materials:

> *"Insistimos en la estrategia de la prevención combinada, en la PrEP y en la PEP (profilaxis pre exposición y profilaxis post exposición) y en la adherencia al tratamiento para que más personas que viven con el virus alcancen el estado de indetectables (es decir, con una carga viral tan baja que no pueden transmitir VIH) para que pronto el VIH deje de ser un problema de salud pública en Paraguay."*

Translation: We insist on the combination-prevention strategy, on PrEP and PEP (pre-exposure prophylaxis and post-exposure prophylaxis), and on treatment adherence so that more people living with the virus reach undetectable status (i.e., with a viral load so low they cannot transmit HIV) so that HIV soon ceases to be a public-health problem in Paraguay.

Source: http://somosgay.org/noticias/detalles/asuntos
