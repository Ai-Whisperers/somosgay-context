# Liderazgo actual

> **TL;DR:** Paloma Vera is Executive Director (verified 2023–2025 ABC Color coverage). Simón Cazal is the co-founder and long-time public face, but his current formal role is unclear post-2024. Clínica Kunu'u is run by coordinator Ramón Martínez with Dr. Carlos Santa María as family physician. Multiple unnamed staff + volunteers.

---

## Named leadership

### Paloma Vera — Executive Director (since at least 2023)

- **Title per ABC Color:** *"titular de la dirección ejecutiva de Somosgay"*
- **Appearances:** ABC Color 2023-11-20, ABC Color 2023-12-01, ABC Color 2025-02-13, TikTok @somosgayorg interview
- **Public stance:** Direct, technical, public-health-focused. Speaks about PrEP, HIV prevention, LGBTIfobia. Less politically combative than Cazal in press (Cazal handles the marriage-equality political front)
- **Source:** https://www.abc.com.py/nacionales/2023/11/20/lanzan-campana-del-dia-de-la-respuesta-al-vih-yo-amo-prep-yo-amo-mas-seguro/

### Simón Cazal — Co-founder, founding Executive Director, public face

- **Born:** 1980, Villeta, Paraguay
- **Religion:** Openly atheist
- **Status:** First public figure in Paraguay to marry someone of the same sex (Rosario, AR, 23 March 2012, with Sergio López)
- **Roles historically:** Executive Director (cited as such in amfAR, Washington Blade, ABC), now appears in 2024 ABC Color interview as the org's spokesperson without the formal ED title — suggesting a transition
- **Personal note:** Married to **Sergio López**, also a Somosgay activist
- **Source:** https://es.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sim%C3%B3n_Cazal
- **Current status as of 2026-07-10:** **[VERIFY]** — last formal press appearance: ABC Color 2024-06-28 (interview about marriage equality), LA Times en Español 2024-09-03
