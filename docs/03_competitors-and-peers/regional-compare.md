# Regional comparison — LATAM community-clinic sites (round 2 — verified)

> **TL;DR:** Fundación Huésped (Argentina) is the strongest LATAM analog by far — 36 years old, free rapid HIV + syphilis testing, full service menu, corporate partner program since 2009. DIKÉ (El Salvador) is closest by vertical (trans-specific). SOMOSGAY's current site is below the LATAM median.

---

## Fundación Huésped (Argentina) — strongest LATAM analog

| | |
|---|---|
| Site | https://huesped.org.ar/ |
| Founded | **1989** (37 years) |
| Type | Fundación (non-profit foundation) |
| Location | Pje. Ángel Peluffo 3932 C1202ABB, Buenos Aires, Argentina |
| Phone/WhatsApp booking | +54 11 6468-1673 |
| Mission | *"Trabajar bajo la perspectiva de Derechos Humanos contra el VIH/SIDA, otras enfermedades transmisibles y en salud sexual y reproductiva"* |
| Tagline | *"Te necesitamos más que nunca"* |
| Staff headcount | 100+ |
| Daily consultations | **100+ daily** (via social, email, phone, in-person) |

### Services (free, all)

1. **Centro de testeo de VIH y Sífilis** — Mon-Fri 10-12 + 13-17, finger-prick rapid test, 20-min results, accompanied by specialized team
2. **Atención personalizada** — first-listening team for general inquiries (HIV, sexual health, access to health, stigma/discrimination cases)
3. **Asesoramiento legal** — for cases of rights violations (access to medication, PEP, etc.)
4. **Apoyo psicológico** — focused therapy for HIV+ and trans people (transition process, diagnosis acceptance, family accompaniment, treatment adherence)
5. **Grupos de pares** — periodic meetings for similar-experience people
6. **Encuentros abiertos a la comunidad** — community meetings
7. **Formación** (training) — corporate + community
8. **Program for empresas** (since 2009) — workplace diversity programs; 2025 sustainability program launched

### What to steal for SOMOSGAY

- **Service menu with accordion** — clear, scannable
- **Free testing as flagship CTA** — *"Centro de testeo"* at top of nav
- **WhatsApp booking prominent** — every page
- **Multi-channel consultation** (social, email, phone, in-person)
- **Mental health + legal aid** as core services
- **Corporate partnership program** — for sustainability (SOMOSGAY could explore this)
- **Patient stories** (likely on their site)
- **Research publications** (they publish research too)

## DIKÉ (El Salvador) — closest trans-specific clinic

| | |
|---|---|
| Founded | 2014 |
| Type | Trans-specific clinic + community centre |
| Started | 2014 |
| Patients on hormones | ~150 |
| Total patients served | >100 |
| Funded by | International Rescue Committee (IRC) |

### Services

- Hormonal treatment for trans women + trans men
- Counseling rooms
- Talk rooms
- Dormitories (transitional housing)
- Kitchen (meals)
- Receptionist + clinical specialists + psychologists
- Public health regulator endorsed + national drug directorate authorised

### What to steal for SOMOSGAY

- Multi-functional centre model (medical + counseling + housing + food)
- Explicit anti-office vibe (*"desecha el concepto de oficina"*)
- Public-private partnership with international NGO
- Transparent patient count
- Public health regulator endorsement

## LATAM site quality spectrum (informal assessment)

| Tier | Sites |

SOMOSGAY is currently in the bottom tier. The rebuild should target top-tier.

## Common LATAM LGBTQ+ org site patterns

- Spanish-primary
- "Quiénes somos" + "Servicios" + "Noticias" + "Contacto" nav
- Donation CTA (usually one button)
- Facebook + Instagram + Twitter prominent
- Rarely have online service booking
- Rarely have multilingual options
- Rarely have full audit transparency (SOMOSGAY is unusual here)
