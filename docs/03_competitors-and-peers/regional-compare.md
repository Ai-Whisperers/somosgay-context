## What LATAM sites typically have

- Spanish-primary
- Mostly news + mission statement + donation CTA
- Rarely have online service booking
- Rarely have multilingual options (most PY/SP sites are Spanish-only)
- Rarely have full audit transparency

---

## What SOMOSGAY's current site does better than peers

- Bilingual nav footer (some English)
- Public audit reports (rare in the region)
- TikTok presence
- Direct WhatsApp contact

---

## What SOMOSGAY's current site does worse than peers

- No service booking
- No events calendar
- No patient stories
- No multilingual UI switch (Spanish/English/Guaraní)
- No app-store integration for Equis
