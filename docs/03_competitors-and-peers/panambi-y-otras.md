### Programs

#### Casa Diversa (since Feb 2019)

- **Type:** First trans shelter in Paraguay
- **Location:** San Lorenzo, 13km from Asunción
- **Capacity growth:** 3 (2019) → 30 (2022)
- **Originally:** Transitional shelter for LGBT+ people (especially trans) facing family violence or homelessness
- **Building:** 2 rooms, 1 bathroom, 1 living room, 1 kitchen, 1 patio
- **Funding:** All donated; 5 organizers cover housing costs from pockets
- **Activities:** Makeup classes (Mondays), theater + visual arts (Saturdays), planned pastry + manicure classes
- **Mission:** "Give the companions a chance. Trainings promote economic autonomy through social inclusion"

#### Transvivienda (since 2022)

- **Type:** Casa Diversa expansion specifically for **adultas mayores trans** (older trans adults)
- **Why:** Discrimination and violence often drive older trans women into homelessness or sex work; no dignified housing options
- **Capacity:** Built on top of Casa Diversa's foundation
- **Sources:**
  - https://agenciapresentes.org/2022/02/09/dulce-hogar-transvivienda-la-casa-digna-para-adultas-mayores-trans-en-paraguay/
  - https://www.pikaramagazine.com/2022/02/transvivienda-una-casa-digna-para-adultas-mayores-trans-en-paraguay/

## Other Paraguay LGBTQ+ orgs

### AIREANA (lesbian / dissident identities)

- **Site:** https://www.aireana.org.py/
- **Mission:** *"Grupo feminista que trabaja por los derechos de las lesbianas y de todas las personas en disidencia sexual y genérica para que vivamos en libertad y sin violencia nuestras sexualidades e identidades."*
- **Objectives:**
  1. Contribute to cultural change via educational + artistic spaces
  2. Collaborate in emancipation of sexually/gender dissident people + strengthen groups
  3. Influence legislation + public policy at national + international level
  4. Strengthen collective care + self-care as group philosophy
- **Annual LGBT film festival** (since ~2005)
- **Tech:** WordPress
- **Sister org to SOMOSGAY** (per IBA 2019)
