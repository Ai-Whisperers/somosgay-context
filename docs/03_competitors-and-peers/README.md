## How to use this folder

- The "competitors" framing is wrong here — these are **peers**, not competitors. SOMOSGAY is the largest LGBTQ+ health-and-rights org in Paraguay; the people who go to AIREANA, Panambi, or The Center (NYC) are not stealing market share from SOMOSGAY.
- We use peer comparison to:
  1. Borrow site patterns (CenterLink, LA LGBT Center, Whitman-Walker)
  2. Understand the regional context (AIREANA, Panambi)
  3. Identify partnership + cross-promotion opportunities
