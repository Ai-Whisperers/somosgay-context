# AIREANA — Paraguay's first lesbian org (round 2 — verified)

> **TL;DR:** AIREANA is alive at www.aireana.org.py (WordPress). Feminist group for lesbian rights + all sexual/gender dissident people. Active sister org to SOMOSGAY. Annual LGBT film festival. Co-organises Marcha del Orgullo.

## Mission (from their site)

> *"Grupo feminista que trabaja por los derechos de las lesbianas y de todas las personas en disidencia sexual y genérica para que vivamos en libertad y sin violencia nuestras sexualidades e identidades."*

Translation: A feminist group that works for the rights of lesbians and all sexually/gender-dissident people so that we can live our sexualities and identities in freedom and without violence.

---

## Objectives (verbatim from site)

1. **Contribuir a un cambio cultural** en la sociedad paraguaya a través de la generación de espacios educativos y artísticos
2. **Colaborar en la emancipación** de las personas disidentes sexuales y genéricas y el fortalecimiento de otros grupos formados por ellas
3. **Incidir en la legislación** y en las políticas públicas en el ámbito nacional e internacional
4. **Fortalecer el cuidado colectivo y el autocuidado** como filosofía grupal

---

## Activities

- **Festival de Cine LGBT** — annual film festival (running since ~2005)
- Joint Pride organisation with SOMOSGAY
- Helpline (per IBA 2019)
- Cultural events
- Political action (per IBA: works with OAS + Inter-American Court of Human Rights)

## Relationship to SOMOSGAY

- Co-organise Marcha del Orgullo
- Co-organise IDAHOBIT + other days
- Share staff / volunteers (IBA article describes a joint visit)
- Shared digital audience — overlap heavily on social media

---

## What this means for the rebuild (round 2 update)

1. **Cross-promote** AIREANA from the SOMOSGAY site (films, events, helpline)
2. **Link out** to AIREANA — both orgs benefit
3. **Avoid duplicating** what AIREANA does well (film festival, lesbian-specific programming)
4. **Joint events calendar** — both orgs list events on both sites
5. **Possible partnership** — shared hosting, shared infrastructure, joint campaigns
6. **Possible multi-tenant** — if Panambi + AIREANA + SOMOSGAY want to share platform, AIREANA's WordPress could migrate to Next.js multi-tenant
