# AIREANA — Paraguay's first lesbian org

> **TL;DR:** Sister org. Paraguay's first lesbian organisation. Co-located in Asunción, co-organises Pride, shares volunteers + advocacy campaigns. Strong film festival tradition (15th annual edition noted by IBA in 2019).

---

## Identity

- **Founded:** 1990s (earlier than SOMOSGAY's 2005 origin)
- **Type:** Lesbian / LGBTQ+ women's rights org
- **Location:** Asunción (own well-furnished, colourfully decorated building in the city centre per IBA)
- **Status:** Active, smaller-scale than SOMOSGAY but with strong cultural-programming legacy

---

## Activities (per IBA Reporter 2019)

- Helpline
- Cultural events — annual **15th LGBT film festival** (running since ~2005)
- Political action — works with OAS and Inter-American Court of Human Rights
- Joint Pride + joint advocacy with SOMOSGAY

---

## Volunteer voices

Per IBA Reporter 2019, **Rosa Posa** (AIREANA volunteer) on whether they need pity:

> *"Don't be pitiful — people here and NGOs are working in spite of society. This is a grassroots operation. People will be ready."*

This frames the regional culture: grassroots, defiant, not asking for charity.

---

## Relationship to SOMOSGAY

- Co-organise Marcha del Orgullo
- Co-organise IDAHOBIT + other days
- Share staff / volunteers (the IBA article describes a joint visit where both orgs hosted)
- Shared digital audience — overlap heavily on social media

---

## What this means for the rebuild

1. **Cross-promote** AIREANA from the SOMOSGAY site (films, events, helpline)
2. **Link out** to AIREANA — both orgs benefit
3. **Avoid duplicating** what AIREANA does well (film festival, lesbian-specific programming)
4. **Joint events calendar** — both orgs list events on both sites
