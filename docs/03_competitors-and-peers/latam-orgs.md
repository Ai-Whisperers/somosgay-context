# LATAM HIV + LGBTQ+ clinic comparative sites (round 2 — verified + extended)

> **TL;DR:** Beyond US benchmarks + DIKÉ, the strongest LATAM analogs are Fundación Huésped (AR), Casa Diversa/Panambi (PY), and AIREANA (PY). SOMOSGAY sits between regional giants (Huésped) and small PY orgs (Panambi). The rebuild should target the Huésped-class UX.

---

## Tier 1 — Major LATAM LGBTQ+ + HIV orgs

### Fundación Huésped (Argentina)

- **Site:** https://huesped.org.ar/
- **Founded:** 1989 (37 years)
- **Location:** Buenos Aires
- **Mission:** HIV/sida + other transmissible diseases + sexual + reproductive health
- **Scale:** 100+ consultations/day, multiple staff
- **Site quality:** Top-tier Spanish-language NGO site
- **Funding:** Public + private + corporate partnership program (since 2009) + 2025 sustainability launch

**Service menu:**
- Free rapid HIV + syphilis testing (finger-prick, 20-min result)
- Personalized attention (multi-channel: social, email, phone, in-person)
- Legal advice
- Psychological support
- Peer groups
- Community meetings
- Corporate diversity training

**Patterns to steal for SOMOSGAY:**
- Service menu as accordion
- Free testing as flagship CTA
- WhatsApp booking prominent
- Multi-channel consultation
- Mental health + legal aid as core services
- Corporate partnership program for sustainability
- Research publications

---

### Casa Diversa / Panambi (Paraguay)

- **Site:** http://www.panambi.org.py/ (basic)
- **Founded:** 2014 (Asociación Panambi) + 2019 (Casa Diversa) + 2022 (Transvivienda)
- **Location:** San Lorenzo (Casa Diversa) + Asunción (Panambi HQ)
- **Mission:** Trans rights + shelter
- **Scale:** 30 residents at Casa Diversa peak (2022)
- **Founder:** Yren Rotela
- **Site quality:** Minimal

**Service menu:**
- Hormonal transition support
- Peer groups
- Skills training (makeup, theater, arts, pastry, manicure)
- Transitional housing
- Income generation

**Patterns to steal for SOMOSGAY:**
- Multi-functional model (medical + counseling + housing + food)
- Skills training as income generation
- Peer-led programming

---

### AIREANA (Paraguay)

- **Site:** https://www.aireana.org.py/
- **Founded:** ~1995 (per IBA Reporter)
- **Location:** Asunción
- **Mission:** Lesbian + dissident identities rights
- **Scale:** Small (WordPress site, no numbers)
- **Tech:** WordPress
- **Annual LGBT film festival**

**Patterns to steal for SOMOSGAY:**
- Festival calendar as content
- Feminist theoretical framing
- Sister-org co-promotion

## What this means for SOMOSGAY rebuild positioning

SOMOSGAY is:
- **Bigger** than Panambi (Casa Diversa), AIREANA
- **Smaller** than Fundación Huésped
- **Different from** Panambi (trans-specific vs general LGBTQ+ + health)
- **Same vertical as** Fundación Huésped (HIV + LGBTQ+ + multi-service)

**Position the rebuild as:** *"Asunción's Fundación Huésped"* — full-service LGBTQ+ + HIV community health with Paraguay-specific Guaraní + tekoporã framing.
