## Visual motifs

### The "108" mark

- 108 rainbow tiles in the youth shelter courtyard fountain
- 108 column paint
- Symbolic number for the LGBTQ+ movement in Paraguay
- Use sparingly and respectfully — it's a memorial to victims of state torture

### The rainbow courtyard

- The youth shelter has columns painted rainbow around a central courtyard
- Rainbow is not decorative — it's memorial + resistance

### The parrot (Paraguayan flag) integration

- Paraguay's flag is red-white-blue, not rainbow
- Combining rainbow with the Paraguayan flag = reclaiming national identity as inclusive
- SOMOSGAY often integrates the rainbow into Paraguayan imagery

### "Tekoporã" framing

- The Guaraní concept of "good living" / "buen vivir"
- Frames the org's work as Paraguayan-rooted, not foreign-imported
- Use as a slogan / value / section header
