## Voice — Paloma Vera (current public-health voice)

From ABC Color 2023-11-20:

> *"Esta estrategia es gratuita y está destinada a varones gays, bisexuales y personas trans, quienes registran tasas más elevadas de infección por VIH en Paraguay. Para una máxima seguridad, se puede combinar con otros métodos de prevención, como el uso de condones y lubricantes a base de agua."*

**Characteristics:**
- Direct, factual
- No euphemism
- Explicit about who the audience is ("gays, bisexuales y personas trans")
- Frames prevention as positive ("combinación", "máxima seguridad")
- Doesn't apologise

## What NOT to do in voice/tone

- ❌ Don't soften or apologise
- ❌ Don't use coded language when explicit language is available
- ❌ Don't use only "LGBT" or only "LGBTI" — match the org's style (they use both LGTBI and LGBTIfobia — see below)
- ❌ Don't talk down to the community
- ❌ Don't use foreign corporate buzzwords ("synergy", "leverage")
- ❌ Don't make the org sound like a generic international NGO — it's Paraguayan-rooted

---

## Tone rules

- **Plain Spanish over jargon.** "Personas que viven con VIH" over "PVVS" or "seropositivos"
- **Active voice.** "SOMOSGAY operates" not "is operated by"
- **Specific numbers over vague claims.** "1,500+ nuevos casos en 2022" not "creciente epidemia"
- **Direct attribution.** Quote sources by name with title
- **Community first.** Lead with the community, not the org
- **Defiant, not pitiful.** Per Rosa Posa (AIREANA): *"Don't be pitiful"*
