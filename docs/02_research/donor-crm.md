# Donor CRM for SOMOSGAY (round 2 — verified)

> **TL;DR:** Recommend **HubSpot for Nonprofits** (free tier) for donor + volunteer + campaign management. Mature, integrates with WhatsApp + Stripe + Gmail + site forms. Best free CRM for small LATAM NGOs in 2025-2026.

---

## Why CRM matters for SOMOSGAY

- Donor pipeline management (acquisition → retention → recurring)
- Volunteer coordination
- Campaign attribution (which channel drove which donation)
- Newsletter segmentation
- Audit transparency (track every donation with timestamp + source + campaign)
- Multi-channel contact (WhatsApp + email + phone + in-person)

## Recommended for SOMOSGAY

### Phase 1 (immediate, $0)
- **HubSpot for Nonprofits** free tier (5 users)
- Migrate any existing donor lists (likely Google Sheets today)
- Set up:
  - Donor pipeline (Lead → First Gift → Recurring Donor → Major Donor)
  - Email templates (welcome, thank-you, recurring confirmation, lapsed reactivation)
  - Forms (donate, volunteer, contact, press inquiry)
  - WhatsApp integration via third party

### Phase 2 (when scaling)
- Upgrade to HubSpot Pro if more users needed
- Add Salesforce NPSP if org needs deep customization
- Cost estimate: $0–100/mo

---

## OPSEC consideration

- HubSpot is US-based
- Donor data hosted on US servers
- Consider Paraguay residency vs US for donor privacy
- For sensitive donors, consider self-hosted CRM (EspoCRM, SuiteCRM)
