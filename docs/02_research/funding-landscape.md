# Funding landscape — Paraguay LGBTQ+ / HIV

> **TL;DR:** SOMOSGAY's funding comes from a mix of international HIV-research funders (amfAR), UN-system agencies (UNAIDS, OPS/OMS), private foundations (AHF), pharma CSR (ViiV Positive Action, via Global Fund), Spanish NGO (Fundación Triángulo), public-sector (MSPBS, PRONASIDA), direct donations (GlobalGiving / Benevity, All Out), and in-kind (DNIT, Cruz Roja). Diversified but grant-cycle dependent.

## Public sector — Paraguay

- **MSPBS** (Ministerio de Salud Pública y Bienestar Social) — top-level health ministry
- **PRONASIDA** (Programa Nacional de Control del VIH/Sida/ITS) — under MSPBS, runs the national HIV program
- **Ministerio de la Mujer** — occasionally supportive (per 2017 GayTimes piece)
- **Ministerio de Educación y Ciencias (MEC)** — **HOSTILE** since 2017 (Resolution 29664)
- **Ministerio del Interior** — cooperation agreement 2009 for police homophobia reduction

---

## Why these funders matter

- **Diversification reduces risk** — not dependent on any single source
- **International funders = international visibility** — easier to raise global profile
- **OPS/OMS endorsement** is a strong legitimacy signal
- **Public-sector partnership** legitimizes service delivery + gives access to hospital network
- **All Out + GlobalGiving** = grassroots individual donor pipeline
- **Pharma CSR (ViiV)** is a niche but reliable source
