# Active grant RFPs for SOMOSGAY-aligned work (round 2 — verified)

> **TL;DR:** Several HIV/LGBTQ+ funders have active or recent RFPs. None are Paraguay-specific (amfAR GMT is Africa-only in 2026), so SOMOSGAY would compete in global or regional pools. Below is the funder-by-funder summary.

---

## amfAR (Foundation for AIDS Research)

**Verified current state:**
- **GMT Initiative Community Awards** — historically supports SOMOSGAY
- 2026 RFPs: **Target Grants** open — Deadline August 24, 2026, 3 PM ET — Up to **$480,000** per grant
- Eligibility: research-focused; community awards are typically smaller
- Source: https://www.amfar.org/research/grants/

**For SOMOSGAY:**
- Existing GMT relationship (since 2012)
- Can apply to amfAR community awards
- Track record with Kuimba'e + Kunu'u + Equis + AMI gives credibility

## Elton John AIDS Foundation (EJAF)

**Verified current state:**
- Open funding calls for HIV testing, prevention, care
- RADIAN initiative (Eastern Europe + Central Asia) — not LATAM-focused currently
- Global funding rounds via application portal
- Source: https://www.eltonjohnaidsfoundation.org/funding/

**For SOMOSGAY:**
- Worth applying; LGBTQ+ + HIV focus matches
- EJAF typically funds mid-size programs

## Global Fund to Fight AIDS, Tuberculosis and Malaria

**Verified current state:**
- Multi-year cycles

- Paraguay CCM exists; SOMOSGAY could be sub-recipient or principal recipient

**For SOMOSGAY:**
- Historical relationship (2014 Equis)
- Likely already part of the PY CCM ecosystem
